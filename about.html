About Me

I was born in West Germany and moved to the US when I was a child. I received a degree in Studio Arts studying ceramics, drawing, painting, and printmaking. I went on to study CommunicationDesign at California College of the Arts, Photography at Rhode Island School of Design, and worked as a Graphic Designer and Advertising Photographer. I've been fortunate to call the San Francisco Bay Area home for more than 23 years.
